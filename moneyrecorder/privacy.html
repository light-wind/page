Money Recorder

Our privacy policy is simple.

We don't collect any of your personal data.

We will never require your name, email or any other personal information. All transactions and other financial information entered in the application are stored on your device locally.

We don't have access to it.

Only aggregate anonymous data about your device and your usage that does not directly identify you is collected. Things such as: version of the app, version of the operating system you are running, color scheme that are you using, number of accounts that you have, etc.

This information is used solely for the purpose of improving our services and is not shared with any 3rd parties.

If you want to delete your data, you can delete the app from your device.
